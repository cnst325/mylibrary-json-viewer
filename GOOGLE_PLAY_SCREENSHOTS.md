## üí° Usage Guidelines

**For Google Play Console:**
1. Upload each screenshot
2. Enter the caption text in the "Caption" or "Screenshot title" field
3. First line typically appears in **larger text**
4. Second line appears in **smaller text**

**Tips:**
- Keep first line under 30 characters for better display
- Make first line action-oriented or feature-focused
- Use second line for additional context or benefits
- Test how captions appear on different screen sizes

---

## üìê Screenshot Dimensions

**Required for Google Play:**
- Phone: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio
- Minimum: 320px on shortest side
- Maximum: 3840px on longest side
- File format: PNG or JPEG (PNG recommended)
- Maximum file size: 8MB per screenshot
